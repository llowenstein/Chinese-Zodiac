The Chinese Zodiac

The Chinese Zodiac is a cycle of 12 years. Each year is represented by a different animal and people born in that year are said to have specific traits related to that animal. Each animal is associated with an element and and has varying levels of compatibility with the other animals.

The Chinese take the zodiac very seriously
