The Year of the Ox

Years include: 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021, 2033...


The ox is a stubborn and cautious animal. People born in this year are honest, industrious, level-headed, and strong-willed. But they can also be obstinate, prudish, and distant.

Lucky numbers:

1, 9

Lucky colors:

Red, Blue, Purple

Associated with:

Earth and Yin

They are very compatible with:

Rat, Monkey, Rooster

They are not compatible with:

Tiger, Dragon, Horse, Sheep
