The Year of the Sheep

Years include: 1919, 1931, 1943, 1955, 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, 2015, 2027, 2039, 2051...


Considered the animal that people like the most. Gentle and calm, their white fur reminds people of beauty. People born in the year of the sheep are polite, kind-hearted, appreciative of art and beauty, and willing to take care of others. However, they can be indecisive, timid, vain, moody, and weak-willed.
