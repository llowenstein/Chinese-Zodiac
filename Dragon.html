The Year of the Dragon

Years include: 1916, 1928, 1940, 1952, 1964, 1976, 1988, 2000, 2012, 2024...


The Chinese believe they are descended from dragons and they are held in high regard. People born in this year are decisive, inspiring, sensitive, and ambitious. However, on the negative side, they can be eccentric, tactless, intolerant, and unrealistic.

Lucky numbers:

1, 6, 7

Lucky colors:

Gold, Silver, Hoary

Associated with:

Fire and Yin

They are very compatible with:

Rat, Tiger, Snake

They are not compatible with:

Ox, Sheep, Dog
