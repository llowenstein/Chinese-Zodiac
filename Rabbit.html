The Year of the Rabbit

Years include: 1915, 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023...


The rabbit is the animal that represents hope. People born in this year are gentle, sensitive, compassionate, and modest. But they can also be amorous, hesitant, and timid.

3, 4, 9

Lucky colors:

Red, Blue, Pink, Purple

Associated with:

Wood and Yin

They are very compatible with:

Sheep, Monkey, Dog, Pig

They are not compatible with:

Snake, Rooster
