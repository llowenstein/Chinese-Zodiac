The year of the Rooster

Years include: 1921, 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, 2029...
Considered the epitome of fidelity and punctuality. In years without alarm clocks,
people relied on roosters a lot. People born in the year of the rooster are considered
bright, honest, and ambitious. They are mostly born pretty or handsome, and may get
excited about things easily. However, they can be impatient, selfish, and narrow minded.
