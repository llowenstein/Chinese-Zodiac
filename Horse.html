The Year of the Horse

Years include: 1918, 1930, 1942, 1954, 1966, 1978, 1990, 2002, 2014, 2026...


The horse is recognized as the Chinese people's ethos, in the sense that it makes efforts to keep improving itself, with passion and diligence. People born in the year of the horse are known for being upbeat and easy-going. Because of these traits, they usually have a lot of friends. However, they are not known for keeping secrets, and love spending money.
