The Year of the Tiger

Years include: 1926, 1938, 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010, 2022, 2034...


Tigers are brave but cruel. People born in this year are said to be tolerant, loyal, valiant, and courageous. However, they can also be arrogant, short-tempered, and hasty.

Lucky numbers:

1, 3, 4

Lucky colors:

Grey, Blue, White, Orange

Associated with:

Wood and Yang

They are very compatible with:

Dragon, Horse, Pig

They are not compatible with:

Ox, Tiger, Snake, Monkey
