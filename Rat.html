The Year of the Rat

Years include: 1912, 1924, 1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020...


The rat is considered to be full of spirit and wit. People born in the year of the rat are said to be adaptable, smart, and cautious. However, they can also be timid, stubborn, picky, and lack persistence.
